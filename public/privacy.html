Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

AgricLife is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect the data you provide when using our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, country of residence.
  • Financial Information: Transaction IDs, payment method details (account numbers for processing only), wallet activity.
  • Usage Data: Login times, pages visited, device and browser information, IP address.
  • Communications: Messages sent to our support team.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To process deposits, withdrawals, and investment transactions.
  • To communicate important updates, confirmations, and support responses.
  • To detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized access.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted password storage, JWT-based authentication, and HTTPS encryption. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and to never share your credentials.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with trusted service providers who assist in platform operations (e.g., database hosting), under strict confidentiality agreements. We may disclose information when required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users.

5. Your Rights

  • You may request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • You may request correction of inaccurate information via your profile settings.
  • You may request deletion of your account by contacting support.

6. Cookies

AgricLife uses session tokens stored in your browser's local storage to maintain your login session. We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

7. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at privacy@agriclife.com.
