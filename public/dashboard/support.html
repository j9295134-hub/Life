Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start investing on AgricLife? ▼
To start investing: (1) Create a free account, (2) Deposit funds into your wallet via MoMo or bank transfer, (3) Browse our stock portfolios and select one that suits you, (4) Click "Invest Now" — your investment becomes active immediately. Returns are credited automatically when the investment matures.
How long does a deposit take to reflect? ▼
Deposits are processed manually by our admin team. After you submit your transaction ID, your wallet is typically credited within 1–6 hours during business hours. If your deposit has not been confirmed after 12 hours, please contact our support team via WhatsApp or Telegram.
When are my investment returns credited? ▼
Returns are credited automatically to your wallet as soon as your investment duration expires. For example, if you invest in a 7-day portfolio today, your principal plus ROI will be in your wallet exactly 7 days from the investment date — no manual action required.
What is the minimum deposit and withdrawal? ▼
The minimum deposit is GH₵40. The minimum withdrawal is GH₵50. There is no maximum limit. Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours of admin confirmation.
How does the referral program work? ▼
Share your unique referral link or code with friends. Every time someone registers on AgricLife using your link, you earn GH₵10 instantly credited to your wallet. There is no limit to how many people you can refer.
Is my investment secure? ▼
AgricLife operates with full transparency. All investment portfolios are managed by certified agricultural professionals. However, as with all investments, there are inherent risks. Please read our Terms & Conditions and Risk Disclosure before investing.
